About the Channel

Aviation content made by someone who actually loves flying

Alpine Flyer is a YouTube channel run from the UK, dedicated to everything aviation. From real-world airline reviews — including short-haul gems like Air Dolomiti across the Alps — to ultra-realistic flight simulation in MSFS 2024, every video is made for people who share a genuine passion for flight.

Whether it's testing how an airline with bad reviews actually holds up, or experiencing an engine failure on takeoff in the simulator, the goal is always the same: honest, engaging aviation content.
